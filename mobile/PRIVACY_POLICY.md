# Privacy Policy for Wedy Business (Merchant App)

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## Introduction

Wedy Business ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our merchant mobile application ("App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you register and use the App:

- **Phone Number**: Required for account registration and authentication via OTP (One-Time Password)
- **Name**: Your full name for account identification
- **Profile Avatar**: Profile picture uploaded by you
- **Business Information**:
  - Business/merchant name
  - Business category
  - Business description
  - Business location and address
  - Cover image (if applicable based on your subscription plan)

### 1.2 Service Information

When you create and manage services through the App, we collect:

- Service name, description, and pricing
- Service category
- Service images (multiple images per service)
- Service location information

### 1.3 Contact Information

We collect contact details you provide:

- Phone numbers (multiple phone numbers allowed based on your subscription plan)
- Social media links (Instagram, Facebook, Telegram, etc.)
- Website URL (if applicable based on your subscription plan)

### 1.4 Payment Information

For subscription and payment processing:

- Payment method selection (Payme, Click, UzumBank)
- Transaction details and payment history
- Subscription plan information and expiration dates

**Note**: We do not store your full payment card details. Payment processing is handled by third-party payment gateways (Payme, Click, UzumBank) in accordance with their privacy policies.

### 1.5 Usage and Analytics Data

We automatically collect certain information about your use of the App:

- User interactions (views, likes, saves, shares of your services)
- Service analytics and metrics
- App usage patterns and features accessed
- Device information (device type, operating system, app version)
- Authentication tokens for session management

### 1.6 Reviews and Ratings

We collect reviews and ratings submitted by clients about your services, including:

- Rating scores
- Review comments
- Review dates

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### 2.1 Account Management
- To create and manage your merchant account
- To authenticate your identity using OTP verification
- To maintain your profile and business information

### 2.2 Service Management
- To enable you to create, edit, and delete services
- To display your services to potential clients
- To manage service images and content

### 2.3 Subscription and Payment Processing
- To process subscription payments through third-party payment gateways
- To manage your subscription plan and enforce usage limits
- To track subscription expiration and renewal

### 2.4 Communication
- To send OTP codes for authentication via SMS
- To display your contact information to clients
- To facilitate communication between you and clients

### 2.5 Analytics and Improvement
- To provide you with analytics about your services (views, interactions)
- To improve the App's functionality and user experience
- To monitor and analyze usage patterns

### 2.6 Legal Compliance
- To comply with applicable laws and regulations
- To respond to legal requests and prevent fraud

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Data Storage

- Your data is stored on secure servers
- Images are stored on AWS S3 cloud storage

### 4.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
- Secure authentication using JWT tokens
- Encrypted local storage for sensitive data

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
